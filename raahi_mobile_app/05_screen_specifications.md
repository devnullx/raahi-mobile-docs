## Design Principles

1. **Big touch targets** - Minimum 48dp, drivers have rough hands
2. **High contrast** - Readable in bright sunlight
3. **Minimal text input** - Use selections, voice, photos
4. **Offline indicators** - Always show sync status
5. **Haptic feedback** - Confirm actions without looking
6. **Multi-language** - Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Marathi, English
